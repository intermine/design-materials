We are an iterdisciplinary team focused on the applications of integrative genomics approaches. Our work ranges from developing open source community resources such as the data warehouse system InterMine, to undertaking and supporting research projects in a range of areas including functional genomics, synthetic biology, and cancer research.

We have a range of student projects available, and are open to collaborations. If you're interested get in touch!
